# Dashboard

Your Dashboard is your home base. It surfaces key stats, quick actions, and recent activity so you can stay on top of what matters.

## Stats Cards

At the top of the Dashboard you'll see summary cards showing key metrics relevant to your tenant. These cards provide a quick overview of your activity and pending items.

> **Tip:** Click a card to jump to the related section.

Here is how a stat card looks:

```preview
component: StatCard
props: { "value": 12, "label": "Team members" }
```

## Quick Actions

Quick actions let you start common tasks without leaving the Dashboard:

- View team members
- Open the **AI Assistant** for a question
- Navigate to admin settings (if you have permissions)

Use these to quickly access the most common workflows.

## Activity Feed

The activity feed shows recent events relevant to you, such as:

- New members joining the tenant
- Configuration changes
- System notifications

Scroll to see more. Use it to stay aware of changes and follow-ups.

### Making the most of your Dashboard

1. **Review stats** — Glance at key metrics to see where things stand.
2. **Use quick actions** — Jump to common tasks without navigating menus.
3. **Check the feed** — Skim recent activity for anything requiring attention.

> **Tip:** Visit your Dashboard regularly to stay informed about your tenant's activity.
