# Member Guide Overview

As a **member**, you can manage your profile, view your dashboard, and interact with the AI assistant. This guide covers what's available to every member of the platform.

## What You Can Do

| Area                   | What's available                            |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **Dashboard**          | Stats, quick actions, activity summary      |
| **Profile & Settings** | Edit profile, avatar, timezone, preferences |
| **AI Assistant**       | Chat for help, search, and recommendations  |

## Quick Links

- [Dashboard](/docs/member/dashboard) — Your home base: stats and quick actions
- [Profile & Settings](/docs/member/profile-settings) — Keep your profile up to date
- [AI Assistant](/docs/member/ai-assistant) — Get help and search via natural language

> **Tip:** Bookmark your Dashboard and use the AI Assistant for quick answers and guidance.
